Guacamole

A simple yet delicious guacamole recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and diced
  • 1 roma (plum) tomato, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice

Directions

  1. Combine the avocado, tomato, onion, salt, and pepper in a bowl and mix.
  2. Mix the lime juice into the guacamole.
  3. Eat with tortilla chips.
