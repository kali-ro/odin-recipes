Guacamole

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and diced
  • 1 roma (plum) tomato, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • 1 serrano chile pepper, seeded and minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 3 drops hot sauce
  • 6 cilantro leaves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice

Directions

  1. Combine the avocado, tomato, onion, serrano chile, salt, pepper, garlic powder, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, and cilantro leaves in a bowl and mix.
  2. Pour lime juice over the top of the guacamole
